Shipping

Shipping and delivery planning for oil paintings

Artwork shipping depends on size, frame status, quantity, destination country, value, urgency, and the level of surface protection required.

Packing method

Paintings can be packed stretched, framed, or rolled depending on the artwork type, size, destination, and buyer requirement.

Lead time

Production and drying time vary by painting complexity, texture level, size, and quantity. Delivery timing is confirmed during quotation.

International delivery

For overseas buyers, we discuss shipping route, packaging needs, documents, and handling expectations before dispatch.

Before shipping

Information that helps us quote accurately

Please share delivery country, preferred deadline, final size, framed or unframed requirement, order quantity, and whether the artwork is for personal use, resale, exhibition, hotel, or public project.
