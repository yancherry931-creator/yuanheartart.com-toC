Process

A clear project workflow

1

Brief

We confirm space type, dimensions, quantity, budget, visual references, and target timing.

2

Direction and quote

We match the brief with suitable artists, production methods, and a practical quotation.

3

Sample or selection

We align on image direction, palette, canvas, finish, framing, and repeat standards.

4

Production and QC

We follow production progress and review artwork completion, color, detail, and packing readiness.

5

Packing and handoff

We prepare the work according to project requirements and coordinate export-ready handoff.

FAQ

Common questions

Can you create work from reference images?

Yes. You can share reference images, room photos, color direction, or a written brief. We will review feasibility and propose a suitable production approach.

Do you support bulk orders?

Yes. Printed oil art, coordinated hand-painted series, and commercial space programs can be planned by quantity, size, budget, and delivery schedule.

Who are your main clients?

We work with Western home decor buyers, design studios, art consultants, museum and gallery teams, hospitality groups, and public art projects.

What should I send for a quote?

Please send dimensions, quantity, intended use, style references, target budget, and desired delivery timing. Irist will confirm the next step.
