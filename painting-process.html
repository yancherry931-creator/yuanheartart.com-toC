Process

How a hand-painted oil painting order is managed

Clear production steps help buyers understand cost, timing, quality checks, and delivery before committing to custom or wholesale artwork.

1

Brief and references

Send size, room context, subject, reference images, quantity, budget, and delivery country.

2

Quote and direction

We align style, material, texture, framing, timing, packing, and shipping expectations.

3

Artist assignment

Through our artist resources, the right painter or production approach is selected.

4

Painting and review

The canvas is painted, checked for visual direction, and prepared for finishing.

5

Drying and finish

Oil paintings require handling time before safe packing and delivery planning.

6

Quality check

We review size, surface, presentation, packing requirements, and order details.

7

Packing

Packaging is selected according to canvas size, frame status, value, and route.

8

Shipping support

We coordinate delivery information and after-sales communication if damage occurs.
