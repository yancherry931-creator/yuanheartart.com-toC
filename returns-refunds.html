After-sales

Returns, damage response, and artwork after-sales support

Custom artwork is reviewed by order scope. We aim to make expectations clear before production and respond quickly if shipping damage or order issues occur.

Custom work

Custom paintings are made to a buyer-approved brief, so standard retail returns may not apply. We confirm important details before production.

Shipping damage

If damage is found on arrival, contact Irist quickly with photos of the package, label, artwork, and affected area for review.

Order review

Resolution depends on order type, evidence, damage cause, shipping route, and agreed project terms.

How to report an issue

Send complete evidence as soon as possible

Include order details, photos of outer packaging, inner protection, the full artwork, close-ups of the issue, and your preferred contact method. This helps us assess the situation efficiently.
