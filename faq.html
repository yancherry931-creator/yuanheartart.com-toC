FAQ

Answers for custom oil painting and wholesale artwork buyers

These answers help homeowners, interior designers, galleries, hotels, museums, and public space buyers understand how to plan artwork with Yuan Heart Art.

Can you make an oil painting from my photo?

Yes. Send the photo, desired size, style notes, and delivery country. We can create a hand-painted interpretation in oil on canvas.

Do you support wholesale orders?

Yes. We support sample orders, repeat series, decor programs, gallery supply, hotel artwork, and commercial project orders.

Can I customize size, color, and frame?

Yes. Size, palette, subject, texture, finish, framing, and series planning can be adjusted around the brief.

Do you supply printed oil art?

Yes. Printed oil art can be useful for larger commercial rollouts, budget-sensitive projects, and coordinated decor programs.

How long does production take?

Timing depends on painting size, detail, texture, quantity, drying needs, and shipping route. Irist confirms timing during quotation.

What should I send for a quote?

Send size, quantity, reference images, style notes, delivery country, deadline, frame requirement, and budget range if available.

Can you work with interior designers?

Yes. We can review room photos, wall dimensions, color palettes, and project schedules for designers and art consultants.

How do you handle shipping damage?

Contact Irist quickly with photos of the packaging, label, full artwork, and damaged area. Each case is reviewed according to order details.

Still have a question?

Send your artwork brief directly to Irist.
