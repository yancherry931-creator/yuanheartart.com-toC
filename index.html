Buyer questions

Quick answers before you request a quote

Can you make oil paintings from a photo?

Yes. We can create custom oil paintings from photos, mood boards, room images, sketches, or artwork references. We confirm size, palette, detail level, and finish before production.

Do you support wholesale artwork orders?

Yes. We support galleries, designers, decor buyers, hotels, and project buyers with sample development, series planning, custom sizes, packing, and export coordination.

Are the paintings hand-painted?

We supply both hand-painted oil paintings and printed oil art. Each quotation can specify the production type, material, size, finish, framing requirement, and expected timing.

How should I start?

Send the subject, dimensions, quantity, reference images, delivery country, target date, and budget range. Irist will reply with the most suitable plan.
