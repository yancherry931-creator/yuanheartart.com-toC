About us

Xiamen Yuanxin Huijing Trading Co., Ltd.

We combine professional artist resources, disciplined trade coordination, and a high-end interior sensibility for global artwork projects.

Brand story

From working studios to international spaces

Yuanxin Huijing serves clients who need more than decorative products. We help buyers translate interior goals, budget, timing, and visual direction into executable artwork programs. Our cooperation with the Putian Oil Painting Association gives us access to first-hand artist resources and helps clients secure a more stable, competitive source of quality paintings.

Our mission

To bring high-quality Chinese oil painting into more Western homes, museums, galleries, hospitality venues, and public art spaces through a clearer, more dependable sourcing experience.
